OUR STORY

Three houses.
One lesson.

You know the story. The first pig built with straw — quick and cheap. The second chose wood — a little better, but still fragile. The third pig took the time to lay every brick with intention. When the wolf came, only one house stood.

That's us. In an industry full of shortcuts, we chose the hard way. We don't rush launches. We don't cut corners on marketing. We don't abandon games after release day.

We build brick by brick, and we build to last. Every game we publish gets the foundation it deserves — because we believe every indie developer's story should end well.
